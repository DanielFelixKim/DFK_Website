Projects

Harmonious Monk

Harmonious Monk (HM) was the final project in my EECS 352 - Machine Perception of Music and Audio class at NU. It allows a user to instantly become a jazz composer by automatically harmonizing speech or a melody. Though pitch tracking and harmonization have been done before, HM presents a different approach: harmonization based on jazz standards. Named after the great Thelonious Monk, HM transcribes speech into pitches and harmonizes it with jazz chords, drawing its chordal vocabulary and harmonic progressions from the tunes it analyzed from the iRealPro database.
